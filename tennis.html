Tennis

Tennis is a racquet sport played individually or in doubles. Players use racquets to hit a ball over a net aiming to score points by landing the ball in the opponent’s court. It requires agility, precision, and stamina. Major tournaments include Wimbledon and the US Open.

Roger Federer is one of the greatest tennis players of all time. Known for his elegance on the court, incredible skill, and sportsmanship,
he has won 20 Grand Slam titles and inspired millions of fans worldwide
